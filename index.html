My learning journey of Web Development

My short journey till now

(Description : Consider this as page of my diary)

First of all , I want you to understand that coding is not easy. The toughest part is to code daily. I belong to a family in which no one understand much about computers. I am the first who is doing Computer Science annd Engg. Before a year ago, I didn't knew the basics of any of the language. My journey began at that time when everyone was into this things.
All my classmates still know a way lot of things and languages than me. So, let me tell you my journey from very zero level.

I first wrote my first code at the time of lockdown. I was in "Second year". I know, I was too late to start.
"C++" was in my Second Semester but that semester was compleated online, that was the time when there was complete lockdown. No exams where taken and I am a sleepy head. I had no interest in coding.

Then , after six months , my life got totally miserable. A friend came to show a hope for me , motivate me to do something rather than just giving up.
He told me about various stuffs related to fields that I can go ahead with. To be very honest : I would had never ever thought of taking a chance into Web dev if he wouldn't pushed me. He was the one who told me about neog.camp
After getting to know about it , I started with first live lesson to another and it never ended. It feels like I got what I wanted the most.

I got impressed by the way Tanay Sir teaches. I had completed all the exercises in the mean time but unfortunately, I didn't make a way to the first batch of the bootcamp because my exams where in Feb- March 2021 , So , I had to study for the semesters.
But I won't miss a second chance , I will make this time count.

During the time gap , I watched enough of the videos related to it and practised it. I also liked some of the Youtube channels name "Code with Harry " , "FreeCodeCamp" etc. I got various certifications too, now I am totally into Web Development. And I am all ready for the 2022 batch neogcamp batch.
